Launchpad

Purpose

The goal of this assignment was to design a tool that could connect users towards a common goal. As a group, we decided to pursue the idea of creating a community around mentor/mentee relationships that would foster an environment of growth, learning, and friendship.

Problem Statement

Community groups around recreational activities can be difficult for some individuals to get integrated into. However, mentorship often bridges the gap between those who are experienced in a skill, and those who are novices and looking to learn and grow in that particular skill. Arguably so, even those who consider themselves novices may be able to provide to someone with no experience, but at least an interest, help and instruction towards learning a new skill.

Experience and knowledge can go so far to give mentors the ability to help others learn. What sets average mentors apart from excellent mentors are the relationships that serves as a foundation between the mentor and the mentee. That is the goal of Launchpad: to foster an environment where mentors not only teach and instruct mentees, but also form relationships with them in order to help them integrate into community groups.

User Studies

The main study group sampled were students at UCSD.

UCSD CSE 170

The coursework assigned was designed to teach students how to create technologies that are pleasant to use. We learned various interaction design techniques such as rapid prototyping and comparative evaluations, and conducted fieldwork with people to come up with ideas for the design.
